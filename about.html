About Kwaanda Labs

Kwaanda Labs is a Rwanda-based innovation lab focused on building local technology solutions. Our mission is to pioneer affordable wireless power systems and deliver hands-on robotics & ICT training to unlock Africa's youth potential.

We’re engineers, educators, and creators — building circuits, developing STEM kits, and training future tech leaders.
